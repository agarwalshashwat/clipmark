Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel my subscription at any time?

Absolutely. If you cancel, your Pro features will remain active until the end of your current billing period. No hidden fees or lock-ins.

How does AI Auto-fill work?

Our AI analyzes the content of the page you're clipping to automatically extract the author, primary topic, and key tags, saving you minutes per clip.

What happens to my clips if I downgrade?

Your data is yours. You will always have access to your existing clips, even if you downgrade to the Free tier. You just won't be able to add more beyond the free limit.

Do you offer educational discounts?

Yes! We support students and educators. Contact our support team with your .edu email for a special discount code.
